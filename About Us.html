History and Background

MSE Study Point was established in 2013 with the mission to provide high-quality coaching and educational support to students. Initially focusing on coaching services, we quickly garnered a reputation for excellence and a commitment to student success.

Evolution and Growth

In 2018, recognizing the evolving needs of our students and the demand for comprehensive educational solutions, MSE Study Point expanded its offerings. We now cater to a wide range of educational courses and services, including undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate (PG) programs, as well as professional certifications.
